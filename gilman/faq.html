Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Gilman Scholarship and how it supports your Kenya Semester Program journey.

+ Who can apply for the Gilman Scholarship?
Any U.S. undergraduate student receiving a Federal Pell Grant is eligible to apply, provided they are in good standing at an accredited institution and participating in a credit-bearing study abroad or internship program.
+ How much funding can I receive?
Awards can be up to $5,000, with an additional $3,000 available for students studying a Critical Need Language such as Swahili.

+ Do I need to speak a foreign language?
No but learning a local language like Swahili while abroad can enrich your experience and strengthen your application.
+ Can I apply earlier than my study abroad semester?
Absolutely! You can apply and receive your Scholarship funds months or around the same time as your initial stuyd abroad start date.
+ Who can I contact for help with my application?
You can contact past KSP Gilman Scholars or the SLU Office of International and Intercultural Studies. Visit the Scholars page for more.
