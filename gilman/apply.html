Apply for the Gilman Scholarship

The Benjamin A. Gilman International Scholarship helps U.S. students study abroad by providing funding to those who might otherwise be unable to afford it. The program supports global education, diversity, and inclusion â€” opening doors to opportunities across the world.

Eligibility Requirements

  • U.S. citizen and undergraduate student in good standing.
  • Receiving a Federal Pell Grant at the time of application or during the study abroad program.
  • Applying to or currently enrolled in a credit-bearing study abroad or internship program.
  • Program must be at least two weeks (for community colleges) or three weeks (for universities).

Application Tips

  • Start early as essays and recommendation letters take time.
  • Reflect on how studying abroad aligns with your academic and career goals.
  • Highlight how you plan to give back to your community after your return.
  • Ask a mentor, professor, or advisor to review your essays.
