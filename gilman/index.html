Gilman Scholars

The Benjamin A. Gilman International Scholarship empowers students to study abroad and experience the world through global education. Meet the scholars who brought that vision to life in Kenya.

What is the Gilman Scholarship?

The Gilman Scholarship provides funding for U.S. undergraduates to study or intern abroad, enabling students of limited financial means to gain international experience and cultural understanding.
